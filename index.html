HOW IT WORKS

1. Light it

Place the candle on the dining table as a beautiful centrepiece.

2. Melt it

As the flame burns, it slowly melts the "candle" into a warm, liquid pool.

3. Dip it

Guests dip pieces of sourdough bread, crackers, or fruits directly into the melted pool.
